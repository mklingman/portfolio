ABOUT

MULTIMEDIA DESIGN STUDENT
KEA Copenhagen School of Design & Technology

Hi, I'm Annemette - a creative soul, who fell in love with coding quite unexpectedly. I've been studying Multimedia Design since January 2020 and it has been a journey. Initially I started studying, because I wanted to utilize my creativity and explore my interest for design and motion.

As time has passed, my interest for coding has grown and I've decided to specialize in frontend design. I find that frontend design has the perfect balance that stimulates my brain. It excites me to plan a good, userfriendly design, bring it to life with code and make websites that are interesting to interact with. These are also the skills I dream to develop further and work with in the future.

In my sparetime I enjoy rollerskating, analogue photography and going to gigs with friends. In summertime I'm usually found reading in a park or listening to music / podcast.
